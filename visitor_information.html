Overview:

  • The Mint Museum is one of North Carolina's oldest art museums, with two campuses:

    1. Mint Museum Uptown — located in the heart of Charlotte's arts district

    2. Mint Museum Randolph — housed in the former U.S. Mint building

  • With a single general admission ticket, you can visit both locations over two consecutive days.
